10. Talk about a festival or special event in your country.

Tet is a tradional festival in Vietnam. A few weeks before Tet, Vietnamese clean their houses and repaint their walls. They also buy new clothes, a peach tree and some things to decorate their houses. One or two days before the festival, people make banh chung and other dishes. On New Year's Eve, the whole family has dinner together and watch the kitchen god. On New Year's morning, young family members Exchange New year’s wishes to elder ,In return, they received lucky money wrapped in small envelopes. After that everyone went to visit neighbors, some friends and relatives.
